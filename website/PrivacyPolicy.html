Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CryptoBal, a Discord bot developed and distributed under the GPL-3.0 license ("the Bot"), collects, uses, and shares information from users who interact with it.

1. Information We Collect
The Bot does not collect any personal information from users, except for information provided by users with their own consent. We do not collect any IP addresses or other device-related information.

2. Use of Information
The Bot uses the information provided by users solely for the purpose of providing its intended services, which are related to cryptocurrency addresses for donations. We do not share, sell, or otherwise distribute user information to third parties.

3. Security
We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of user information. The Bot's servers are located in secure data centers and are protected by firewalls and other security measures. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of user information.

4. Links to Third-Party Websites
The Bot may contain links to third-party websites that are not affiliated with us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting a notice on the Bot. Users are responsible for ensuring that their contact information is up-to-date and accurate.

6. Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@rabbit-company.com.

By using the Bot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to be bound by its terms and conditions.
